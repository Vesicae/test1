Our Story

Born from Passion,
Built for Gamers.

GameStoB started as a small idea between two friends in 2015 — the belief that every gamer deserves access to authentic titles at fair prices, backed by a community that understands the culture. From a single laptop and a handful of digital keys, we've grown into a trusted name with over 5,000 titles and multiple physical store locations across the country.

Our Journey

From a small idea to a thriving gaming ecosystem.

2015

The Beginning

Founded with just 50 titles and a passion for gaming culture. First 100 orders came from a Discord community.

2017

First Physical Store

Opened our first brick-and-mortar location, proving that the gaming retail experience wasn't dead.

2019

Catalog Hits 1,000 Titles

Partnered with major publishers and expanded our digital key platform to cover all platforms.

2022

National Expansion

Expanded to 10 cities, launched a loyalty program, and surpassed 500,000 customers served.

2026

Global Vision

Launching our official global website, bringing GameStoB to gamers worldwide with 5,000+ titles.

Who We Are

More than a store — a community.

GameStoB is a digital and physical game retailer headquartered in Indonesia. We specialize in providing authentic game keys, pre-order titles, DLCs, and collector's editions for PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.

What makes us different is our dual presence — we bridge the gap between the convenience of online shopping and the community experience of physical stores. Our staff are gamers themselves, capable of giving real recommendations based on actual gameplay.

We believe gaming is not just entertainment — it is culture, art, and connection. Our mission is to ensure every gamer, regardless of budget or platform, has access to an authentic and enjoyable experience.
